Hello!

I’m a PhD Research Scholar at Norwegian School of Economics in Bergen and the labor economics section of the Centre for Experimental Research on Fairness, Inequality and Rationality.

I do research in labor economics and economic history, using modern register data and novel sources of individual-level data from the early 20th century to the present. I’m particularly interested in the evolution of inequity and social mobility over time and its causes.
